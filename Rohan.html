Training Approach

Rohan's transformation was achieved through a meticulously designed program that evolved through different phases:

  • Foundation Phase: Focus on compound movements to build overall strength and muscle mass
  • Hypertrophy Phase: Targeted volume training for specific muscle groups needing development
  • First Competition Prep: Strategic combination of resistance training and cardio to preserve muscle while reducing body fat
  • Post-Competition Improvement: Addressing specific areas identified during first competition
  • Advanced Competition Prep: Specialized training to enhance muscle separation, symmetry, and conditioning for Bangabir 2025

Nutrition Strategy

Rohan's nutrition was periodized to support each phase of his transformation:

  • Initial Stage: Moderate caloric surplus with high protein intake
  • Building Phase: Strategic caloric surplus with nutrient timing
  • First Competition Cutting Phase: Calculated deficit with basic carb manipulation
  • Improvement Phase: Nutrition periodization to support targeted growth
  • Bangabir Prep: Advanced cutting with refined carb cycling and precision timing
  • Competition Peak: Specialized carb loading and water manipulation protocol

Lessons From Competition Experience

The progression from Haldia Gold Classic to achieving 4th place at Bangabir 2025 demonstrates several key aspects of successful competitive bodybuilding:

  • Experience Value: Stage time and first competition feedback were invaluable for improvement
  • Refined Approach: Adjustments to training, nutrition, and presentation based on real competition experience
  • Mental Development: Enhanced confidence and stage presence from previous competition experience
  • Strategic Improvements: Targeted development of specific areas based on judge feedback
