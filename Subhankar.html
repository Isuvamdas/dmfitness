Training Approach

Subhankar's transformation was achieved through a scientifically designed program developed by Coach Debasish Mondal:

  • Foundation Phase: Establishing proper technique with basic compound movements and building overall work capacity
  • Hypertrophy Focus: Progressive overload with strategic volume increases targeting balanced muscular development
  • Specialization: Targeted volume for lagging muscle groups to create symmetrical physique
  • Competition Preparation: Balanced approach to resistance training and cardio to preserve muscle while enhancing definition
  • Peak Week Protocol: Specialized training to maximize muscle fullness and appearance for stage presentation

Nutrition Strategy

Subhankar followed a periodized nutrition plan designed and monitored by DM Fitness Studio:

  • Initial Phase: Focus on protein sufficiency and overall food quality while establishing sustainable eating habits
  • Building Phase: Strategic caloric surplus with nutrient timing to support muscle growth
  • Competition Preparation: Progressive caloric adjustment with carbohydrate manipulation to reduce body fat while preserving muscle
  • Final Weeks: Refined manipulation of macronutrients, sodium, and water to enhance muscle definition and fullness
  • Peak Week: Specialized carbohydrate loading protocol to maximize stage appearance
